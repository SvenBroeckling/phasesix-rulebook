    \tcblower
\textit{Flying}: Doldagor can fly from birth, moving freely through the air. They require a high jumping point or running start, plus a successful quickness roll, to take off. Their movement range is doubled while flying. Flying allows for a parallel action, loke 'walking'.

\textit{Dive}: An unarmed strike is possible during a dive. For every five metres of flight distance in the same combat round, the attack gains an additional die. However, for every 10 metres of flight distance in the same combat round, the Doldagor itself suffers a normal hit upon impact.

* \textbf{Small Straight} - The character gains one boost immediately
* \textbf{Full House} - The character gains one destiny die
* \textbf{Large Straight} - The character rediscovers an item in his backpack (the player chooses an item and adds it to his backpack)
* \textbf{Four of a kind} - The character gains +1 actions in every round of the next combat
* \textbf{Five of a kind} - The characters group gains one additional turn prior to the enemies in the next combat

In each case, the highest pattern to be achieved counts for a roll. So 5, 4, 3, 3, 2, 1 is a large straight, but not three of a kind.
